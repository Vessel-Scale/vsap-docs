# Editing Icons

This page provides guidance on editing and managing icons within the assessment library.

## Overview

Icons are used throughout your assessments to enhance visual representation and improve user experience. Learn how to select, customize, and manage icons for your assessment questions and content.

## Icon Management

### Accessing Icon Settings

To edit icons in your assessment:

1. Navigate to the Library section
2. Select the assessment you want to edit
3. Open the question or element you wish to customize
4. Look for the icon selection or editing option

### Icon Selection

Choose from our curated library of icons to best represent your assessment content. Icons help users quickly identify question types and content categories.

### Customizing Icons

Customize icons to match your branding and design preferences:

- **Size**: Adjust icon dimensions to fit your layout
- **Color**: Match icons to your brand colors and color scheme
- **Style**: Select from different icon styles available in the platform

## Best Practices

- Use icons consistently throughout your assessment
- Ensure icons are clearly visible and easily distinguishable
- Match icon choices to the content they represent
- Consider accessibility when selecting icon colors
- Test icon appearance across different screen sizes

## Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues with icon display or editing:

- Clear your browser cache and reload the page
- Try a different browser to rule out compatibility issues
- Check that your icons meet the platform's file size requirements
- Ensure your internet connection is stable during uploads
