# Library

The **Library** section is where you can browse and launch new assessments for your organization. Think of it as the assessment marketplace — your account has access to a curated set of assessments that you can use to evaluate different aspects of your business.

## Accessing the Library

1. Log in to your Client Portal
2. Click **Library** in the left sidebar
3. You'll see all available assessments organized in a searchable, filterable interface

---

## Finding assessments

### Search by name or description

Use the **Search** field at the top to quickly find assessments by name or description.

![Library search field](../assets/screenshots/client-portal/library-search.png)

### Filter by group/category

Many assessments are organized into **Groups** (also called categories). Click the **All Groups** dropdown to filter by a specific category.

- The dropdown only shows groups that have assessments available
- Filtered results update in real-time
- Click **All Groups** to see all available assessments again

![Library group filter](../assets/screenshots/client-portal/library-groups.png)

### Combine search and filters

Use search and group filters together to narrow down your options. For example:
- Search for "Supply Chain" + filter by "Operations" group
- The interface always shows you which filters are active and how many results match

---

## Assessment cards

Each assessment appears as a card showing:

- **Icon** — a visual indicator for quick recognition
- **Name** — the assessment title
- **Description** — what the assessment evaluates
- **Groups** — which categories the assessment belongs to (displayed as small badges)

Click any card to see more details and launch the assessment.

---

## Launching an assessment

1. Click on an assessment card
2. Review the **Assessment Details** modal which shows:
   - Full description
   - Assessment type and version
   - Any relevant notes or context
3. Click **Launch Assessment** or **Start Evaluation** to begin
4. You'll be taken to the assessment questionnaire to start responding

---

## Tips for using the Library

- **New assessments added regularly** — check back often for new assessment options
- **Save time with search** — if you know the assessment name, search for it instead of scrolling
- **Use groups to explore** — browse by group to discover assessments in a specific area (e.g., "HR & Talent," "Financial Resilience")
- **Read descriptions** — assessments may have similar names but evaluate different things, so read the full description before launching

---

## What happens after you launch?

Once you launch an assessment:

1. You'll be taken to the **assessment questionnaire**
2. You can complete it immediately or save your progress and return later
3. Your responses are saved automatically as you work
4. Once submitted, you'll see your results in the **[Completed Surveys](assessments-table.md)** section of your portal
