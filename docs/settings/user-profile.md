# User Profile & Account Settings

Manage your personal account information and security settings.

## Overview

Your user profile contains your personal information, contact details, and password. Access your profile by:

1. Clicking your **avatar** in the top-right corner of the header
2. Selecting **Profile** from the menu

Or navigate directly to the **Profile** page using the URL: `/profile`

---

## Edit Your Information

### Available Fields

Update your personal details in the **My Information** section:

| Field | Description | Required | Notes |
|-------|-------------|----------|-------|
| **First Name** | Your first name | Yes | Displayed in profile and communications |
| **Last Name** | Your last name | Yes | Displayed in profile and communications |
| **Email** | Your email address | Read-only | Contact support to change your email |
| **Profile Image** | Your avatar photo | No | Upload a photo to personalize your profile |
| **Address 1** | Street address | No | Primary address line |
| **Address 2** | Street address (cont.) | No | Apartment, suite, etc. |
| **City** | City or locality | No | Your location |
| **State** | State or province | No | Your location |
| **Country** | Country | No | Your location |
| **ZIP/Postal Code** | Postal code | No | Your location |
| **Website** | Your website URL | No | Personal or company website |

### How to Update

1. Click your **avatar** in the header
2. Select **Profile**
3. Edit any fields in the **My Information** section
4. Click **Save** to apply changes
5. The page will confirm the update

!!! info "Email Changes"
    Your email address cannot be changed through the profile editor. If you need to update your email, please contact your administrator or support team.

### Profile Image

Personalize your profile with a custom avatar:

1. Click the **Change Image** button or your current avatar
2. Select an image file from your computer
3. The platform will automatically resize and optimize the image
4. Click **Save** to apply changes

**Supported formats:** PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP  
**Recommended size:** 200×200 pixels

## Change Your Password

### Security Best Practices

Protect your account by using a strong password:

- Use at least 8 characters
- Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Avoid common words or personal information
- Use a unique password (don't reuse from other sites)
- Change your password regularly (every 3–6 months)

### How to Change Password

1. Click your **avatar** in the header
2. Select **Profile**
3. Scroll to the **Change Password** section
4. Enter your current password for verification
5. Enter your new password
6. Confirm the new password by re-entering it
7. Click **Save** to apply changes
8. The page will confirm the update

!!! warning "Current Password Required"
    You must provide your current password to change it. This protects your account from unauthorized changes.

### Password Requirements

- **Minimum length:** 8 characters
- **New password must differ** from your current password
- **Confirmation:** New password must match in both fields
- **Case sensitive:** Passwords are case-sensitive

### Troubleshooting

**"Current password is incorrect"**
- Ensure caps lock is not on
- Verify you're entering the correct password
- Password is case-sensitive

**"New passwords don't match"**
- Re-enter your new password in both fields
- Ensure they are identical

**"Password change failed"**
- Try again or contact support if the issue persists

## Access Restrictions

### Who Can Edit Their Profile

**Regular users** can always edit their own profile information.

**Administrators and staff members** can edit their profile if their role has permission to do so. Some administrative accounts may have their profile editing restricted based on organization policy.

### Staff Profile Restrictions

If you're unable to access the profile page despite being logged in:

- Your organization may have restricted profile editing for staff/superuser accounts
- Contact your system administrator for assistance
- You may still be able to view your information, but edits may require admin intervention

## Tips & Best Practices

- **Keep information current** — Update your profile with current contact details so colleagues can reach you
- **Professional photo** — Use a clear, professional profile photo for better team recognition
- **Privacy consideration** — Only enter information you're comfortable sharing with other users
- **Password safety** — Never share your password with anyone, including administrators
- **Backup contact** — Add alternate address or website if available
- **Regular updates** — Review your profile every 6 months to ensure all information is current
